About Us

# Section Description
1 What is the Problem? Explains what issues people face when trying to buy or rent boats.
2 Proposed Solution Describes how our website solves these issues easily and safely.
3 Why It's Better Shows why our idea is better than general marketplaces.

1. What is the Problem?

Many people love boats but find it hard to buy, sell, or rent them online. On normal websites, boat listings are mixed with cars, bikes, and other items, making it confusing and time-consuming. Sellers can't reach their real audience, and buyers waste time searching through unrelated ads.

Also, there's no system for safe communication or trusted delivery between users. This makes transactions risky and less reliable. Renting boats is even harder — most sites are not built for short-term rentals, and people don't know who to trust.

2. What is the Proposed Solution?

Our idea is to create Boat Haraj — a platform focused only on boats. It combines all actions related to boats in one simple website: buying, selling, renting, and even delivering.

  • Buy & Sell: Post ads with clear photos, location, and price.
  • Rent: Short or long-term options with schedules and conditions.
  • Delivery: Optional delivery requests with automatic cost calculation.
  • Search & Filters: Filter by boat type, size, location, and price range.
  • Chat: Built-in chat for direct communication.
  • Ratings: Feedback system for users and delivery partners.

Everything happens in one trusted and focused environment designed for the boating community.

3. Why It's Better?

Boat Haraj is different because it focuses only on boats. This specialization makes the platform more efficient, reliable, and easy to use.

  • Focus: No mixed results — only boats.
  • All-in-One: Buy, sell, rent, and deliver easily.
  • Quick Search: Find what you need in seconds with filters.
  • Trust: Verified users and ratings create safety.
  • Simplicity: Designed for all users, even beginners.
